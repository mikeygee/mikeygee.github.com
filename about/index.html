My name is Michael "Mikey" Gee (pronounced like the letter G). I am a software engineer in the Los Angeles area, where I have lived my entire life.

I discovered computer programming when I was 18 and knew right away that I had found my calling. There is something magical about writing code and watching it work, that fascinates me more than anything else. I love building apps and learning new software technologies. I currently specialize in front end web development, building web sites and applications with HTML/CSS and Javascript programming.

In my free time, I love to get outside and be active. I enjoy pretty much all sports, but my favorites are basketball, snowboarding, running, and cycling. I also don't mind staying indoors and watching movies, TV shows, and my favorite LA sports teams (Lakers, Kings, UCLA Bruins).

My philosophy on life can be summarized in 3 L's - learn, love, and laugh. I strive to challenge and improve myself a little each day, treat people with love and respect, and find ways to laugh and enjoy the little things. Conan O'Brien said it best, "Work hard, be kind, and amazing things will happen."
